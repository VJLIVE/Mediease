About Us

MEDIEASE is a cutting-edge project developed by Team Quantums as part of the Gemini API Hackathon. Our website leverages the powerful Gemini API to enhance healthcare through innovative technology, delivering a seamless and user-friendly experience. Our project aligns with the Sustainable Development Goal of "Good Health and Well-Being," reflecting our commitment to improving health outcomes through advanced web development.


Technologies We Use:

  • HTML: For structuring our content.

  • CSS: To ensure a visually appealing design.

  • JavaScript: For interactive features.

  • Firebase: To power our backend services.

  • Gemini API: To power our Mediease AI.

  • Flask (Python): To integrate Gemini API with the website.

Mediease AI Integration:

To provide you with the best possible support on your health and fitness journey, we have integrated the Gemini API into our platform. This powerful tool allows us to deliver personalized responses on topics related to nutrition, diet, and physical fitness.

How It Works:

  • Personalized Responses: Ask questions about nutrition, diet, or physical fitness, and receive tailored answers based on the latest data and research.
  • Real-Time Assistance: Get instant, up-to-date information to help you make informed health decisions.
  • Accurate and Reliable: Trust the information provided by our advanced algorithms and extensive research.
